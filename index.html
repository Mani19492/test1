Ask Question?

Hack-Attack

How do I register for the hackathon?
To register, go to our official website and click on the 'Register Now' button. Fill out the required information, and once you're successfully registered, you’ll receive a confirmation email
Do I need coding experience to participate?
No, you don’t need extensive coding experience. Hackathons are for everyone, including beginners. There will be mentors to help you, and workshops to build your skills."
Can I participate if I’m a beginner?
Absolutely! Hackathons are a great way to learn and grow. Many participants are beginners, and we have resources to support you throughout the event.
What should I bring to the hackathon?
Bring your laptop, charger, any necessary ID, and a notebook. If you have any other equipment you’re comfortable with, feel free to bring that as well.
How long does the hackathon last?
The hackathon is a 24-hour event, starting from 6th friday 10 am to 7th Saturday 2pm. Make sure to pace yourself and take breaks!"
Can I form a team with friends, or will I be assigned one?
You can form a team with friends beforehand or find team members at the event. We also have team-building activities to help you find like-minded participants.
What if I get stuck on a problem?
Mentors will be available throughout the hackathon to help you with any technical or non-technical issues. Don’t hesitate to ask for help!
Are there any prizes for winners?
Yes, we have exciting prizes for the top teams, 1.2 l cash prizes, and internships from DRDO and TASK opportunities with our sponsors.
